Why it matters

Scam tokens and honeypots are everywhere

A token can look liquid and active while quietly taxing sellers, concentrating supply, or blocking sells entirely. Token Trust Score is the pre-trade gate that aggregates the signals that matter.

Honeypot & tax risk
